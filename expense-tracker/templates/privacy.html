Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you before any sharing takes place.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
